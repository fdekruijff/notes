# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Tech peers and recruiters browsing from desktop and mobile who want a quick read on my technical depth, writing quality, and current interests.

## Product Purpose
This site publishes technical writing and personal insights in a format that is easy to scan and pleasant to read, while making it easy for visitors to follow updates and contact me.
Success means visitors read multiple posts, return for new writing, and can quickly reach out through social/contact channels.

## Brand Personality
Precise, warm, opinionated.
Voice should feel clear and thoughtful without sounding sterile, and should convey confidence through structure and readability.

## Anti-references
Avoid startup-gradient SaaS aesthetics and generic conversion-focused landing-page tropes.
Avoid sterile corporate dashboard styling that sacrifices personality and editorial warmth.

## Design Principles
1. Reading-first hierarchy: headline, metadata, and body rhythm should support deep reading before decoration.
2. Credible clarity: visual choices should signal technical rigor and trustworthiness.
3. Warm precision: use restrained personality cues that humanize the interface without adding noise.
4. Progressive depth: surface summaries quickly, then reward deeper exploration with clear structure.
5. Durable accessibility: preserve legibility, contrast, and motion comfort across all pages and devices.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Use sensible accessibility defaults with high legibility and keyboard-friendly interaction.
Respect reduced-motion preferences for non-essential animation.
Prioritize strong text/background contrast and readable typography across themes.
